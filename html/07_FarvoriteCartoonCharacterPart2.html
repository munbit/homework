About Doraemon

"Doraemon" or simply Doraemon is the main, titular character of all Doraemon media (including, but not limited to the original manga, the anime [1973, 1979, and 2005], video games, and movies). Doraemon's signature colour is blue and he is usually represented by the colour blue.
